FAQs

What is Frontend Mentor, and how will it help me?

−

Frontend Mentor offers realistic coding challenges to help developers improve their frontend coding skills with projects in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. It's suitable for all levels and ideal for portfolio building.

Is Frontend Mentor free?

+

Frontend Mentor offers both free and premium challenges. The free tier provides access to a wide variety of projects, while the premium subscription unlocks additional complex challenges and resources.

Can I use Frontend Mentor projects in my portfolio?

+

Yes! Frontend Mentor projects are designed to be used in your portfolio. We encourage users to customize and add their own creative touch to the challenges to showcase their unique skills to potential employers.

How can I get help if I'm stuck on a challenge?

+

Frontend Mentor has a supportive community where you can ask questions and get feedback. You can post questions in the dedicated Slack channel, Discord server, or directly on the challenge page to receive help from fellow developers.
